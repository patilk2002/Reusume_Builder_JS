HOW DO I MAKE A FIRST RESUME ?

Maximize the impact of our online resume builder with a clean, uncomplicated layout that’s easy to scan, and perfect for automated screening. The average hiring manager only looks at a resume for an average of six seconds. Using a simple resume design helps you make those seconds count!

+
WHAT SHOULD BE THE PERFECT TILTLE OF THE RESUME ?

A good resume title often includes your target job title, your key skills, your qualifications, and/or your years of experience. You can also include your awards, industry, or specializations

+
WHAT IS THE DIFFRENCE BETWEEN RESUME AND CV ?

A resume is a brief summary of your skills and experience over one or two pages, a CV is more detailed and can stretch well beyond two pages. The resume will be tailored to each position whereas the CV will stay put and any changes will be in the cover letter.
